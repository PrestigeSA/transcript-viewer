For the vehicle shop I have two main motivations for wanting to own it: 1. I think that it can be a pretty powerful tool, and can also have a very strong effect on the economy, so having someone who understands that, and doesn't want to screw up the entire economy for their own benefit I think will go a long way. 2. Too often vehicle shops and the RP arround the vehicle shops are very one dimensional. I would love to take the ownership of the business seriously, but use the power and story that comes with a shop to further deepen RP levels that are directly related to vehicles. Examples I thought of while wanting to own the shop: Providing legal documents for those who enquire/pay for boosting/thefts that permits a work order from a legal business. This creates an underground vehicle economy, a reliable source of good RP between owner and criminal, and if done correctly, the ability for FIB/PD to scope out what's going on. I think its also super important to know that I'd be creating a new character to suit this role, and that I will not be scared to risk things in order to provide the RP - if it means the character loses his business becuase of good police work then thats the type of angle Im looking to take. Another idea would be to provide individuals a backstory/ability to VIN scratch a vehicle/wash their money through a legitimate business. I think for a lot of cities vehicle dealerships are very one dimensional of get stock, sell cars, and as much as that will be part of what we do, I'd love to create other alleys to explore everything to do with vehicles. Im not too sure what other info you'd require or if you want me to create a short proposal that you can look over, just let me know
